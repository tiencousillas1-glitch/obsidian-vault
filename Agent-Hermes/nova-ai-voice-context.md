## Core Mission
Build and sell AI voice agents, starting with orthodontic/dental clinics in the US.
Primary principle: **If it does not move revenue forward, it is noise.**

---

## Nova AI Voice — The Business

**Brand:** Nova AI Voice (exact, no variations)
**Niche:** Orthodontic and dental clinics (US market)
**Pain:** Clinics lose patients from missed calls. Problem is conversion at the phone, not demand.
**Offer:** AI voice assistant that answers, qualifies, and books appointments 24/7

**Positioning:**
> I help orthodontists stop losing patients from missed calls through Nova AI Voice, an AI voice assistant that books appointments automatically.

**Tagline:** Clear Value, Clear Bookings

**CTA:** "Talk to Chloe" (demo assistant, not "Book an appointment")

**Pricing:**
- Setup fee: $300
- 14-day free trial
- Monthly: $297 / $497 / $700 (tiers)

**Transformation pillars:**
1. Call capture and qualification (name, phone, email, patient status, reason, treatment interest, urgency)
2. Controlled scheduling (respects clinic hours, availability, requires info before booking)
3. Confirmation and follow-through (date, time, location, in-person nature, contact details)

**Enemies of the offer:**
- Missed calls, front-desk chaos, manual follow-up inconsistency, overworked receptionists, generic scheduling tools, DIY automations that don't understand clinic workflows

**Common objections + answers in document.**

## Target Client Profile

Orthodontists 35-60, single or multi-location, $1.5M-$2.5M/year revenue, 5-8 employees per orthodontist.

They care about: practice growth, reputation, patient trust, full schedule, better call handling, real booked consultations, less front-desk stress, clear ROI.

---

## Sales Strategy

Channels: Cold email, cold calls, landing page demos, Facebook/LinkedIn outreach, dental vendor referrals.

Execution bias: Do not wait for perfect market. Pick a segment, ship a demo, send outreach, learn from replies.
