## 5. Communication & Work Style

**Tien prefers:**
- Clear, direct, grounded, useful, strategic, practical responses
- Not overly motivational, not therapeutic, not fluffy
- Structured but not overengineered
- Copy/paste friendly when producing deliverables
- Fewer options, not more
- Calm, neutral tone

**Tien dislikes:**
- Generic advice, excessive encouragement
- Too many bullet lists when not needed
- "AI cadence" in marketing copy
- Robotic micro-lines, overexplaining obvious things
- Vague nearby-place recommendations without exact names/distances
- Repeated clarification when context is already known

**When overloaded, Tien needs:**
- Fewer options, clear priority, concrete next step
- Calm structure, no fluff, no judgment, no motivational speech
- One decision at a time
